About Asanga Mobility

Driving Bhutan's Electric Future

Asanga Mobility

Asanga Mobility is the sole authorized distributor of BYD electric vehicles in Bhutan. We are committed to bringing world-class electric mobility solutions to the Bhutanese market.

With a focus on sustainability and innovation, we aim to contribute to Bhutan's vision of maintaining its carbon-negative status while providing cutting-edge transportation options to the people of Bhutan.

Our partnership with BYD, a global leader in electric vehicles, enables us to offer premium electric cars with advanced technology, exceptional range, and superior build quality.

About BYD

BYD (Build Your Dreams) is a leading high-tech enterprise specializing in the automotive, rail transit, new energy, and electronics industries. Since its establishment in 1995, BYD has grown to become a global leader in electric vehicles.

With innovative technology and a commitment to sustainable development, BYD offers comprehensive solutions for zero-emission transportation, including passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and rail systems.

As a pioneer in new energy solutions, BYD is dedicated to creating a better world by bringing clean, efficient, and affordable mobility to all.
